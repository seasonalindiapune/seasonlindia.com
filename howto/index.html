1. Health Benefits of Jaggery Powder

  • Rich in Minerals: Unlike refined sugar, jaggery retains iron, magnesium, and potassium.
  • Digestive Aid: It activates digestive enzymes and helps in preventing constipation.
  • Immunity Booster: Packed with antioxidants that help fight oxidative stress.
  • Energy Sustenance: Provides a slower release of energy, preventing sudden blood sugar spikes.

2. How to Use for Tea

  1. Prepare a fresh cup of hot brewed tea.
  2. Open one BareCane Jaggery Powder sachet and pour it into the cup.
  3. Stir the tea thoroughly using a small spoon until the powder dissolves completely.
  4. Your healthy, jaggery-sweetened tea is ready to enjoy!
