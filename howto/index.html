Guide for BareCane Jaggery Powder

How to Use BareCane Jaggery Powder for Tea and Everyday Recipes

Discover easy, natural ways to enjoy jaggery powder in tea and other recipes. This guide covers the best preparation methods, benefits, and inspiration for your kitchen.

Health benefits

  • Rich in minerals: retains iron, magnesium, and potassium.
  • Supports digestion: helps maintain a balanced digestive system.
  • Natural energy: provides sustained sweetness without refined sugar.

How to use for tea

  1. Boil fresh water and steep your preferred tea leaves or tea bag.
  2. Pour the hot tea into a cup and add one or two teaspoons of BareCane Jaggery Powder.
  3. Stir gently until the jaggery dissolves completely.
  4. Enjoy the warm, smooth sweetness with natural nutrients.
